Reset front end session
=======================

If you have problems with your front end session, e.g. the shop basket
always loses its products or you want to get rid of a logged in user,
then you should reset the front end session.

Browser
-------

You must clear the cookie from your browser. Open your preferences of
the browser window and remove the cookie of your TYPO3 front end
session. Search for the url of your website where you can see a cookie
www.my-typo3-domain.com fe_typo_user. Delete this cookie and your FE
session is cleared an repaired.
